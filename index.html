What is Cloud Skills Challenge ?

Make sure you are equipped with the knowledge and skills to stay relevant in this ever-changing industry. "Cloud Skills Challenge" will be a self-paced competition for Undergraduates of University of Ruhuna.

Happening from 1st of February 2021 to 1st of April 2021 in which you would have to complete modules through the Microsoft Learn Platform. Points would be given for each module you complete throughout the given period. Collect enough points to stay in the Top of the Leader board to win Exclusive Swags from Microsoft!

*NOTE: THIS CHALLENGE IS ONLY APPLICABLE FOR THE STUDENTS OF UNIVERSITY OF RUHUNA

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my Display Name?

You can edit your Display Name on the Edit Your Profile Page. Make sure that your Display Name is above naming convention provided by us. Any other display names will be invalid for the competition

When will be the Registrations end?

Participants can register untill the end of the event date.

Can other students join?

No. This is an inner university challenge organized by university of ruhuna. Only students of university of ruhuna can join this challenge.
